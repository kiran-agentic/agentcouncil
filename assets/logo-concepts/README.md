# Logo Concept Options

These are early symbol-only logo concepts for AgentCouncil.

- `concept-01-constellation-council.svg`: multiple agents arranged as a council around a shared intelligence core
- `concept-02-braided-reasoning.svg`: independent reasoning streams weaving into one decision
- `concept-03-council-chamber.svg`: the most literal council metaphor, with abstract agents around a shared chamber
- `concept-04-orbit-assembly.svg`: a more technical, systems-oriented assembly around a central intelligence engine

The goal here is concept selection, not final branding. Shapes, palette, stroke weight, and refinement can all change once one direction feels right.
